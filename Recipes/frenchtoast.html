French Toast Recipe

Quick and easy french toast recipe perfect for any morning and only requiring a few staple ingredients that are likely already in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 6 slices white bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix egg, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, sugar and salt with a wire whisk in a bowl
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly
  3. Cook break slices on a lightly greased nonstock grittle or pan on both sides until lightly browned
  4. Serve with maple syrup and top with berries if desired
